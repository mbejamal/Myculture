Who We Are

We are a passionate cultural enterprise dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Northern Ghana through traditional dance, drumming, and immersive cultural experiences. Founded and managed by Mr. Jamal-Deen Yakubu, our organization offers authentic dance instruction, cultural performances, and guided tours that connect visitors and locals alike to the deep-rooted traditions of our people. To ensure our tourists' interests are fully met, we collaborate with reputable institutions and skilled cultural practitioners, combining expertise and local knowledge to deliver meaningful, engaging, and memorable experiences. We believe in the power of culture to inspire, educate, and unite communities. With every beat of the drum and every step of the dance, we celebrate identity, history, and the vibrant spirit of Northern Ghana.
