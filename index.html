Who We Are

Northern Ghana Art and Cultural Dance Organisation is a vibrant cultural enterprise dedicated to preserving, promoting, and celebrating the rich traditions of Northern Ghana. Rooted in our deep heritage, we offer a wide range of services including the teaching of traditional dance and drumming, guided cultural tours, and captivating live performances. Our mission is to keep our ancestral rhythms alive while sharing the beauty, history, and spirit of Northern Ghana with the world. Through education, performance, and community engagement, we aim to inspire pride, unity, and cultural appreciation across generations.
